不过多介绍AIGC的基本内容

主要介绍如何精确画出想要的部分、达到自己想要的效果

最好的操作就是 上手 多练 参考 模仿 修改 调整

出图的关键:选词 + 反向关键词 + 合理调整操作(U/V)

很多英文prompt与中文有所差别,因此要留意用词

第一天生成的图:

1. 2D Animation Scene

"Create a vibrant 2D animation scene featuring a whimsical forest with anthropomorphic animals having a picnic. The style should be colorful and cartoonish, with expressive characters enjoying various picnic foods. Think of bright, sunny lighting and a background that's rich in details like trees, flowers, and a small stream."

2. Industrial Design of a Futuristic Car

"Design a futuristic car with sleek, aerodynamic lines and eco-friendly technology. The car should look advanced yet elegant, with solar panels integrated into the roof and transparent, touch-sensitive control panels. Imagine it parked in a smart city environment, reflecting the latest in automotive design and sustainability."

3. UI/UX Design for a Mobile App

"Sketch the UI/UX design of a mobile app that helps users track their daily water intake. The interface should be minimalist and user-friendly, with a calming color scheme. Include a dashboard showing daily goals, intake history, and hydration tips. Use icons and visual elements that make the app intuitive for all ages."(no realistic phone border or model of mobile phone)

4. Digital Painting of a Fantasy Landscape

"Create a digital painting of a breathtaking fantasy landscape. Imagine towering mountains with crystal-clear waterfalls, a glowing forest filled with ancient trees, and a sky illuminated by two moons. The scene should evoke a sense of wonder and magical atmosphere, with a path leading into the heart of the forest."

5. 3D Model of a Robot Companion

"Design a 3D model of a small robot companion designed for household assistance. The robot should have a friendly and approachable appearance, with modular parts for different tasks like cleaning, cooking, and security. Visualize it in a modern home setting, showcasing its functionality and compact design."

6. VR Experience of an Underwater Exploration

"Imagine a VR experience that takes users on an exploration of a coral reef under the sea. The scene should be teeming with colorful marine life, including schools of tropical fish, gentle sea turtles, and curious dolphins. Highlight the interactive elements, like hidden treasures or environmental conservation tasks, to engage users."

7. Game Environment Concept Art

"Concept art for a game environment set in a post-apocalyptic city. The scene should blend nature's reclamation of urban spaces, with overgrown buildings and streets reclaimed by flora and fauna. Incorporate elements of survival and exploration, with hidden resources and safe havens among the ruins."

8. Fashion Design Sketch for a Futuristic Outfit

"Sketch a futuristic outfit design that blends high fashion with advanced technology. Imagine a garment with smart fabrics that change color based on the wearer's mood and integrated flexible screens displaying dynamic patterns. The silhouette should be avant-garde, reflecting a blend of elegance and innovation."
